## Subscribing

The changelog is published as an RSS feed at `/changelog.xml`. Drop that
URL into any feed reader to get release notes the moment they ship.

## What you'll see

Each entry maps to one release post. Titles are short, summaries are
the first paragraph of the post, and the link points back to the
canonical entry on the docs site.

## Email

If RSS isn't your style, the same content goes out by email on a
weekly cadence. Subscribe from the [Changelog](/changelog) header.
