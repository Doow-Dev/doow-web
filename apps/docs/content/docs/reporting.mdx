## What's built in

Three rollups ship with every workspace: **spend by category**, **spend
by owner**, and **renewals in the next 90 days**. Each is exportable as
CSV or PDF and can be sent on a schedule.

## Custom rollups

Custom rollups are defined by a small filter expression: a date range, a
field, and an aggregation. The query is saved to your workspace and can
be reused.

## Scheduled exports

Reports can be sent on a Monday-morning cadence to a Slack channel or an
email distribution list. The schedule is the same surface as approvals —
configurable per-workspace.
