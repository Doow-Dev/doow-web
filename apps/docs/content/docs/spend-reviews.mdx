## The cadence

A spend review is a recurring sweep where the reviewer checks every
transaction that crossed a threshold or fired a rule. Most teams run it
monthly; some do weekly during budget season.

## What gets surfaced

Doow surfaces three buckets: **unowned spend** (no internal owner),
**off-policy spend** (matched a rule), and **untagged spend** (missing a
category). Reviewers triage each bucket in under fifteen minutes when
the workspace is well-classified.

## Output

A review produces three artefacts: a queue of fixes, a CSV for finance,
and a delta against the previous period. The delta is the most useful for
exec readouts.
