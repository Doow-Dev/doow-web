Connect your Zoho account to sync your organization's user directory into Doow.

> **Note:** This integration covers Zoho as an **identity provider** — syncing users managed through Zoho's directory. If you use Zoho People for HR, see the [Zoho People HRIS integration](/integrations/hris/zoho-people).

## What you need

- A Zoho account with **Administrator** privileges in your Zoho organization

## What Doow reads

### Users
- Full name and primary work email address
- Account status (active or inactive)
- Department and job title (where set in your Zoho profile)

## What Doow does not read

- Passwords or authentication secrets
- CRM data, email content, or any product-specific Zoho data
- Any data outside user profiles and account status

## How to connect

1. Go to **Company Settings → Integrations** in your Doow workspace
2. Find **Zoho** and click **Connect**
3. A popup window opens — Doow fetches the Zoho OAuth consent URL
4. The popup navigates to Zoho's OAuth consent screen
5. Sign in with an Administrator account
6. Review the requested permissions and click **Accept**
7. The popup closes automatically and Doow completes the connection
8. A success notification confirms the integration is connected

> If your browser blocks the popup, you will be redirected to Zoho's consent screen directly. After approving, you are redirected back to the Integrations page and the connection completes automatically.

## Permissions requested

Doow requests the following Zoho OAuth scopes:

| Scope | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `ZohoDirectory.Users.READ` | Read user profiles and account status |

All scopes are read-only. Doow cannot create, modify, or delete any data in your Zoho account.

## Confirm the sync worked

After connecting, open the integration detail page from **Company Settings → Integrations**. A healthy identity sync should show a connected state, a recent sync timestamp, imported users, and app assignment or activity records where the provider makes them available.

If the connection is successful but no users appear, verify that you connected the right tenant, granted admin consent, and allowed the required read scopes.

## Troubleshooting

Use these checks when the connection succeeds but data is missing or incomplete:

- Confirm you connected the intended tenant or domain.
- Confirm the admin consent screen granted the requested read permissions.
- Confirm the provider account can read users, groups, and app assignments.
- Check the integration event log for permission or sync errors.
- Trigger a manual sync when the integration supports it.

## Disconnecting

To disconnect, go to **Company Settings → Integrations**, find Zoho, and click **Disconnect**. You can also revoke the Doow app from your Zoho account under **My Account → Connected Apps**.

## Next steps

After the sync works, connect HRIS if you need departments and employment status. If you are reviewing AI, cloud, or app usage, connect a usage data source next.
