Connect Zoho People when Doow needs employee records, departments, job titles,
and employment status from Zoho People.

<Callout title="Zoho People is separate from Zoho identity">
  Use this page for Zoho People as an HR system. If you use Zoho as a login or
  directory provider, use the [Zoho identity integration](/integrations/identity/zoho).
</Callout>

## What you need

- A Zoho People account that manages the employees Doow needs to read.
- A Zoho People admin who can approve the HRIS connection.

<Callout title="Use employee-form read access">
  Zoho People APIs expose employee data through People forms and OAuth scopes.
  Doow needs read access to employee records, not access to leave, attendance,
  payroll, or performance modules.
</Callout>

## What Doow reads

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Employee name | First and last name |
| Work email | Primary work email used to match employees to app users |
| Department | Department or team where Zoho People returns it |
| Manager | Reporting manager where available |
| Job title | Role, designation, or job title where Zoho People returns it |
| Employment status | Active, inactive, terminated, or similar status values where available |
| Start date / end date | Date of joining and termination date where available |

For a full description of how this data is used in Doow, see [HRIS overview](/integrations/hris).

## How to connect

1. Go to **Company Settings**, then **Integrations** in your Doow workspace.
2. Find **Zoho People** and select **Connect**.
3. Complete the secure Zoho People linking flow.
4. Sign in with the Zoho People admin account that owns approval.
5. Approve read access for the employee records Doow imports.
6. Wait for Doow to verify the connection and begin the initial employee sync.

## Permissions requested

Doow requests Zoho People read access for employee records:

| Scope | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `ZOHOPEOPLE.forms.READ` | Read employee form records and selected employee fields |

Zoho People scopes define the service, module, and operation type that an app
can use. Doow uses read access for employee reporting fields only.

## What Doow does not read

- Salary, compensation, payroll, or reimbursement data
- Leave balances, attendance records, or timesheets
- Performance appraisals or HR notes
- Tax IDs or personal government IDs
- Zoho Mail, CRM, Books, Desk, or other Zoho product data

## Confirm the sync worked

After connecting, open the integration detail page from **Company Settings**,
then **Integrations**. A healthy Zoho People sync shows a connected state, a
recent sync timestamp, and imported employee records with work email,
department, job title, and employment status where Zoho People returns them.

If records are missing, confirm that the connected Zoho People admin can read
the employees Doow needs and that those employees have work email addresses.

## Troubleshooting

Use these checks when Zoho People employee data is missing or stale:

- Confirm the connected Zoho People admin can read the expected employee
  records.
- Confirm employee work email addresses are present and match identity records.
- Check whether inactive or terminated employees are filtered by Zoho People
  permissions, views, or sync settings.
- Check the integration event log for permission or sync errors.

### Zoho People-specific checks

| Symptom | Likely cause | Next action |
|---|---|---|
| Employees do not appear | The OAuth grant belongs to the wrong Zoho account or the admin cannot read employee records | Disconnect and reconnect with the intended Zoho People admin |
| Department, role, or status is missing | The employee form field is not populated or not visible to the connected admin | Confirm the field exists in Zoho People and is visible in employee records |
| Consent fails or expires | The Zoho People OAuth grant was denied, expired, or revoked | Reconnect Zoho People and approve the employee-form read scope |

## Disconnecting

Go to **Company Settings**, then **Integrations**, find Zoho People, and select
**Disconnect**. Doow deletes the stored authorization immediately. Revoke the
Doow app from Zoho Accounts under **Sessions**, then **Connected Apps** when
you want to remove provider-side access too.

## Next steps

After Zoho People employees appear, connect an identity provider if Doow needs
to match employees to app users, groups, and assignments.
