Connect BambooHR to sync your employee roster and organizational structure into Doow.

## What you need

- A BambooHR account with **Admin** access
- Your BambooHR company subdomain (for example, `yourcompany.bamboohr.com`)

## What Doow reads

- Employee name, work email, department, job title
- Employment status (active, terminated)
- Start date and termination date

For a full description of how this data is used in Doow, see [HRIS overview](/integrations/hris).

## How to connect

1. Go to **Company Settings → Integrations** in your Doow workspace
2. Find **BambooHR** and click **Connect**
3. A secure linking modal opens
4. BambooHR is pre-selected in the modal — log in with your BambooHR Admin credentials within the modal
5. Approve read-only access when prompted
6. The modal closes and Doow completes the connection and begins the initial employee sync

## What Doow does not read

Salary, compensation, payroll data, performance reviews, tax IDs, or any financial HR data. See [HRIS overview → What Doow does not read](/integrations/hris#what-doow-does-not-read).

## Confirm the sync worked

After connecting, open the integration detail page from **Company Settings → Integrations**. A healthy HRIS sync should show a connected state, a recent sync timestamp, and imported employee records with work email and employment status.

If records are missing, confirm that the connected HR account has access to the employees you expect Doow to read and that those employees have work email addresses.

## Troubleshooting

Use these checks when employee data is missing or stale:

- Confirm the connected HR admin can read the expected employee records.
- Confirm employee work email addresses are present and match identity records.
- Check whether terminated or inactive employees are filtered in the provider.
- Check the integration event log for permission or sync errors.

## Disconnecting

Go to **Company Settings → Integrations**, find BambooHR, and click **Disconnect**. You can also revoke access from your BambooHR account under **Settings → API Keys**.

## Next steps

After the sync works, connect an identity provider if you have not already done so. Identity data helps Doow match HRIS employees to app users and assignments.
