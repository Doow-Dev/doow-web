## Why ownership

Spend without an owner becomes spend without a decision. Every paid app
in Doow has exactly one **owner** and an optional **backup reviewer**.
When renewal questions land, they go to the owner first.

## Auto-assignment

For new apps Doow auto-assigns the heaviest user as a candidate owner.
The candidate is just a suggestion — workspace admins confirm before
anything is fixed.

## Backup reviewers

Backup reviewers handle approvals when the owner is unavailable. Adding a
backup is optional but recommended for any app with monthly spend over
$1,000.
