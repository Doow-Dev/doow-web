## Built-in roles

Three roles ship with every workspace: **owner** (full read and write),
**reviewer** (read everything, write reviews and approvals), and
**viewer** (read-only). Most teams stay on these three.

## Scopes

Each role resolves to a set of scopes. Scopes are the unit the API
checks — for example, `transactions:write` or `approvals:read`. The full
scope list is exposed in the audit log.

## Custom roles

Custom roles are available on the business plan. They compose existing
scopes; you cannot define a new scope.

## Audit

Every permission change is recorded in the audit log, including who
made the change and the prior value.
