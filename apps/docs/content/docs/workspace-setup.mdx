## Identity and billing

A workspace pairs one identity provider with one billing surface. SSO is
optional during trial but required before production access. Billing can
be wired to a Stripe customer or to a procurement-managed invoice path.

## Roles

The default roles cover most teams: **owner**, **reviewer**, and
**viewer**. Custom scopes are available on the business plan; see
[Permissions](/permissions) for the matrix.

## Workspace settings

Workspace-level settings live under Settings → Workspace. Most fields are
safe to change after launch — the exception is the workspace slug, which
is part of every shareable URL.
