## Who to invite first

Start with one **owner** per business area, the **reviewer** who runs your
spend cadence, and any **viewers** who need read-only access for
finance close. You can add more later — invitations are reversible.

## Invitation flow

Invitations are sent by email. Recipients accept and pick a workspace
display name. SSO-enforced workspaces skip the password step entirely.

## Bulk invites

For larger rollouts, paste a list of emails into the bulk invite field
or upload a CSV. Each row maps to one role.
