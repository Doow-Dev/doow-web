Doow helps your team map software spend — connecting the tools your organization uses to give you a clear picture of usage, cost, and who's actually using what.

## What you can do with Doow

- **Track usage** across SaaS tools, cloud platforms, and internal services
- **Map spend** by connecting your identity provider, HRIS, and billing sources
- **Identify waste** by seeing which licenses are unused or underutilized
- **Make informed decisions** about renewals, consolidations, and budget allocation

## How this help center works

Articles are organized into five categories:

- **Getting started** — workspace setup and your first steps
- **Integrations** — connecting data sources
- **Billing & plans** — invoices, plans, and payments
- **Account & settings** — team and workspace management
- **Troubleshooting** — fixing common issues

Use the search bar at the top to find any article by keyword.

## Getting support

If you can't find what you're looking for, our support team is here to help.

- Click **Chat with us** in any article to start a live chat
