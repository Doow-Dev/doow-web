You can update your payment method at any time from Billing settings. Changes apply to the next invoice.

## Replace your credit card

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment method**.
2. Click **Update card**.
3. Enter your new card number, expiry, and CVC.
4. Click **Save**.

The new card becomes the default for all future charges. Your previous card is removed.

## Add a backup payment method

Doow supports one active payment method per workspace. If you need to change cards without a lapse in service, add the new card first, then remove the old one.

## Update billing address

Your billing address appears on invoices and is used for tax calculations.

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Billing details**.
2. Edit the address fields.
3. Click **Save changes**.

## Add a VAT number

If your organization is VAT-registered, adding your VAT number removes VAT from future invoices.

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Billing details**.
2. Enter your VAT number in the **Tax ID** field.
3. Click **Save changes**.

Doow validates VAT numbers against official registries. If validation fails, check that the number matches your registration certificate exactly.

## Failed payments

If a payment fails, Doow will:
1. Retry the charge after 3 days, then again after 7 days.
2. Email the billing contact with instructions to update the payment method.
3. After the final retry, downgrade the workspace to read-only.

To restore access after a failed payment, update your card and contact [support@doow.co](mailto:support@doow.co) to manually retry the charge.
