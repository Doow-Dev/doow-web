Doow billing is seat-based. You're charged for the number of active members in your workspace, billed monthly or annually depending on your plan.

## What's on your invoice

Each invoice includes:

- **Plan name** — the tier you're on (Starter, Growth, or Enterprise)
- **Billing period** — the month or year covered
- **Seat count** — the number of active workspace members at the time of billing
- **Unit price** — per-seat price for your plan
- **Subtotal** — seat count × unit price
- **Tax** — applied based on your billing country and VAT number if provided
- **Total due**

## Finding past invoices

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Scroll to the **Invoice history** section.
3. Click any invoice to download the PDF.

Invoices are also emailed to the billing contact address on file.

## Billing contact

The billing contact receives invoice emails and can be different from the workspace owner. To update it:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Billing details**.
2. Edit the **Billing email** field.
3. Save changes.

## Proration

If you add seats mid-cycle, the additional seats are prorated for the remaining days in the billing period. The prorated amount appears as a separate line item on your next invoice.

## Questions about a charge

If you see a charge you don't recognize or believe there's an error, [chat with us](#) or email [support@doow.co](mailto:support@doow.co) with your invoice number.
