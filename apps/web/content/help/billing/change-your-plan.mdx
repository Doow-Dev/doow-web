You can change your Doow plan at any time from the Billing settings page. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

## Upgrade your plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Change plan**.
3. Select the plan you want to upgrade to.
4. Review the new pricing and click **Confirm upgrade**.

Your card will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing period at the new plan rate.

## Downgrade your plan

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Change plan**.
3. Select a lower tier and click **Confirm downgrade**.

The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll retain access to your current plan's features until then.

### What changes when you downgrade

Some features available on higher tiers are restricted on lower tiers. Before confirming a downgrade, Doow will show you a list of features that will become unavailable.

## Convert a trial to paid

When your trial ends, you'll be prompted to enter payment details to continue. You can also convert early:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Add payment method** and enter your card details.
3. Select the plan you want and click **Subscribe**.

Your trial data and integrations are preserved when you convert.

## Cancel your subscription

To cancel, go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription**. Your workspace remains active until the end of the paid period. After that, it moves to a read-only state — you can still view historical data but cannot add integrations or invite new members.
