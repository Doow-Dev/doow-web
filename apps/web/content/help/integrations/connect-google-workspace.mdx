Connecting Google Workspace gives Doow access to your user directory, group memberships, and (optionally) app usage signals from Google's Admin Reports API.

## What you'll need

- Google Workspace **Super Admin** account
- Doow workspace with **Admin** role

## Steps

1. In Doow, go to **Integrations** and find **Google Workspace**.
2. Click **Connect**.
3. You'll be redirected to Google's OAuth consent screen.
4. Sign in with your Super Admin account.
5. Review the requested permissions and click **Allow**.
6. You'll be redirected back to Doow. The first sync starts automatically.

## Permissions requested

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `admin.directory.user.readonly` | Read user accounts and profile data |
| `admin.directory.group.readonly` | Read group memberships |
| `admin.reports.audit.readonly` | Read app usage events (optional) |

## After connecting

- Users from your Google Workspace directory will appear in **People**.
- Group memberships are used to assign users to teams in Doow.
- If you granted the Reports API scope, app usage events will populate within 24 hours.

## Troubleshooting

If the connection fails, confirm that:
- You signed in with a Super Admin account (not a delegated admin)
- Your Google Workspace plan includes the Admin SDK API (available on Business and Enterprise plans)
